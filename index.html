Popular Cities in the Peach State

City population patterns in the Georgia

Three popular cities in the Peach State, Georgia, are Atlanta, Athens, and Savannah. All are popular destinations. Atlanta is the capital of Georgia with a large population of 486,290. Athens is a college town, home to the Univeristy of Georgia. While the city is smaller, it attracts people with its vibrant music and art scene.
